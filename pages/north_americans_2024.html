About This Event

The Corinthian Yacht Club of Seattle and the Port of Seattle's Shilshole Bay Marina welcome you to Washington state and the beautiful Puget Sound for the 2026 J/105 North Americans. The event's debut in the Pacific Northwest will pair world class racing with one of the most popular sailing venues in North America.

In addition to racing, the event will include a number of Northwest themed social functions including a unique Opening Ceremony, can't miss post-race refreshments, and daily awards and a Closing Ceremony.
