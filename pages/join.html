Welcome to the Fleet!

Fleet 22 Lake Erie is a vibrant community of J/105 sailors who share a passion for competitive one-design racing, camaraderie, and sailing excellence on Lake Erie. Whether you're a seasoned racer or new to the J/105 class, we welcome you to join our fleet.

Membership Benefits

  • Competitive Racing: Participate in local and regional J/105 regattas
  • Community Events: Social gatherings, seminars, and fleet meetings
  • Fleet Resources: Access to technical support, measurement, and racing documents
  • Member Directory: Connect with fellow J/105 owners and crew
  • Website & Communications: Fleet news, regatta results, and announcements
  • National Events: Eligibility for J/105 North Americans and other championships
  • Sponsorship: Fleet-sponsored social events at regattas

Requirements

To join Fleet 22, you need:
  • Own or have access to a J/105

    Boat must be registered and in good standing

  • Be located on or near Lake Erie

    Current fleet ports: Cleveland, Buffalo, Toledo, Erie

  • Pay annual dues (see below)

    Two separate payments: Class Association + Fleet 22

Looking to crew?
Even if you don't own a J/105, we can connect you with boat owners looking for crew. Contact us to get started!

How to Join - Simple Steps

1 Pay J/105 Class Association Dues

Amount: $100 per year

What it's for: National J/105 Class Association membership, class rules, sail certification, and national championships eligibility

Pay J/105 Class Dues

Payments processed by J/105 Class Association

2 Pay Fleet 22 Lake Erie Dues

Amount: $50 per year

What it's for: Local Fleet 22 activities, website maintenance, regatta support, and social events
