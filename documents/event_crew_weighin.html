Crew Weigh-In Data

For the weigh-in process at the 216 Regatta:
Boats can declare their crew weights using the spreadsheet. However, we will have the official scale, which will be used for the North Americans, set up at the event. This will allow everyone an opportunity to check their weigh-in results, address any discrepancies for the 216 event and give preparation for the North Americans. This is designed to give crews a chance to ensure they are in compliance.
