About Fleet 22 Lake Erie

Currently, Fleet 22 Lake Erie boasts over 17 registered yachts located around Lake Erie. Our vibrant community of sailors hail from various ports, including Cleveland, Ohio; Buffalo, New York; and Toledo, Ohio. We are passionate about sailing and fostering a spirited, friendly competition among our members.
